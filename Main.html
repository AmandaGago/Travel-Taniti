Taniti

Where Life Could be a dream

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific, covering less than 500 square miles yet offering an incredible variety of landscapes, from white sandy and rocky beaches to lush rainforests and a mountainous interior featuring a small, active volcano. Home to an indigenous population of about 20,000, Taniti has traditionally relied on fishing and agriculture, but recent growth in tourism has brought new opportunities and experiences for visitors. Taniti City, with its native architecture and beaches along Yellow Leaf Bay, serves as the main hub for sightseeing, while Merriton Landing on the north side of the bay has rapidly developed into a center for entertainment, dining, and lodging.

Visitors to Taniti enjoy a wide range of activities and amenities, including ten restaurants serving local, American, and Pan-Asian cuisine, multiple grocery options, and lodging that spans from budget hostels to four-star resorts, all strictly regulated by the government. Adventure and leisure abound, from snorkeling, rainforest hikes, and volcano tours to cultural attractions like museums and art galleries, as well as nightlife options such as pubs, a dance club, and a microbrewery. The island is easily accessible by air, with plans to expand the airport for larger jets, and ground transportation is convenient with public and private buses, taxis, rental cars, and bicycles. With its stunning scenery, welcoming community, and wide variety of activities, Taniti offers a tropical escape where every visitor can find something unforgettable.

Getting to and around Taniti is simple and convenient. Most visitors arrive by air at the small airport, which accommodates jets and propeller planes and is being expanded to welcome larger aircraft. A weekly cruise ship also docks in Yellow Leaf Bay. Once on the island, tourists can rely on public buses in Taniti City, private buses across the island, taxis, rental cars, or bicycles. Taniti City is walkable, especially around Merriton Landing, making it easy to explore on foot. With its breathtaking scenery, welcoming community, and endless activities, Taniti is a tropical escape where every visitor can create unforgettable memories.
